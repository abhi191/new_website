Lucid Capacity

Lucid Capacity is an internal SAP tool to manage workforce and capacity within the company.
With this tool the capacity management of employees is fun and easy! Employee-managers have a clear overview about their employees and what they are working on. Offering an easy way to maintain their employees, create / staff teams and identifying allocation gaps. Managers can get a clear overview of employees working in projects, in teams, the duration of the project and the percentage allocation of time for project.
They can also manage their employees, give substitution rights to Operation People to manage on their behalf and get reports out of this.
